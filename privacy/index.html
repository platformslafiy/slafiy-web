Privacy Policy

Effective: April 20, 2026
Last updated: April 20, 2026

Overview

Slafiy ("we", "our", or "us") operates mobile applications and digital services (the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Summary: We collect only what is needed for our multiplayer features (authentication, game state, chat). We do not sell your data, show ads, or use tracking for advertising.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information (optional, for online features)

If you choose to use online multiplayer features in our games, you sign in with Google Sign-In. When you do, we receive:

  • Email address (used to create a unique account)
  • Google account display name (used only during authentication; replaced by your chosen nickname inside the game)
  • Google user ID (internal identifier)

You can play all single-player (AI) modes without signing in or providing any personal information.

2. Gameplay Data

For online multiplayer, we store:

  • Your chosen nickname (not your real name)
  • Game state (board position, moves, scores) during active matches
  • Chat messages you send during a match
  • Match timestamps

This data is stored on Google Firebase Realtime Database and is necessary for real-time multiplayer to function. Match data is deleted when the match is deleted by the user.

3. Purchase Data

In-app purchases (such as premium board unlocks) are processed entirely by Google Play Billing or Apple App Store. We receive only a confirmation that a purchase was made — we do not access or store payment card details.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos (except when you explicitly save a screenshot), microphone, or camera
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA, AAID) — we do not show ads
  • Third-party analytics for advertising
  • Browsing history outside the app

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure (Google Sign-In)
  • To enable real-time multiplayer matches between you and other players
  • To deliver in-game chat messages to your opponent
  • To process in-app purchases (via Google/Apple)
  • To respond to support requests you send us

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Realtime Database) — hosts your online match data. Firebase Privacy
  • Google Play Services — for Google Sign-In and Play Billing on Android
  • Apple Sign-In & App Store — for authentication and billing on iOS (if you use Apple ID)

Our apps also let you share results via WhatsApp, Telegram, X, or Instagram. This sharing is optional and initiated by you; content is then handled by those platforms under their own policies.

Chat Messages

Chat messages sent during an online match are stored temporarily on our servers so that both players can see them. You are responsible for the content you send. We do not actively moderate chat content but reserve the right to remove messages or accounts that violate our terms (harassment, hate speech, spam). You can report abusive behavior by emailing support.

Data Retention

  • Active match data is kept while the match is ongoing.
  • Finished matches remain in your account history until you delete them.
  • Chat messages are deleted when the associated match is deleted.
  • Account data is retained while your account is active. You can request account deletion at any time (see Contact).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent by signing out and uninstalling the app
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@slafiy.com.

Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for a general audience but may include optional chat features. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your region). If a parent or guardian believes their child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Data Security

We use Google Firebase, an industry-standard infrastructure that encrypts data in transit (TLS) and at rest. However, no system is 100% secure, and we encourage you to use a strong Google/Apple account password and enable two-factor authentication.

International Data Transfer

Slafiy is based in Turkey. Our data infrastructure (Firebase) is operated by Google and may store data in data centers located in the European Union or the United States. By using our Apps, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. For significant changes, we will provide notice within the app. Continued use of our Apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play online with a friend?
Open the app, tap Online Mode, sign in with Google, then tap New Game and choose Private Room. Share the invite link with your friend. They'll be taken directly into the game when they tap it.
What are Public Rooms?
Public Rooms let you create a game anyone can join, or join a room someone else has opened. It's the fastest way to find an opponent when your friends aren't online. Each user can have one open public room at a time.
Can I change my nickname?
Yes. Go to the home screen and tap the nickname card (with the pencil icon) to edit it. Your nickname is what opponents see in online matches — your real name is never shared.
How do in-app purchases work?
Premium board sizes (8×8 and 16×16) are unlocked with a one-time purchase. Payment is processed by Google Play or the App Store — we don't see your payment info. If you reinstall, use the Restore Purchases button in the purchase screen.
My purchase didn't go through
Purchase issues are usually resolved by Google or Apple directly:
  • Android: Open Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Budget & history
  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions or reportaproblem.apple.com
If the purchase is confirmed on Google/Apple but the feature isn't unlocked in the app, tap Restore Purchases. If that doesn't work, email us with your order ID.
My opponent isn't responding
SOS Game supports async play — your opponent can take their turn whenever they want, even hours later. If a match stays inactive too long, you can delete it from the My Games list by tapping the three-dot menu.
Someone sent an abusive message in chat
We take this seriously. Please email us at support@slafiy.com with:
  • Your nickname
  • The opponent's nickname
  • A screenshot of the message
  • The approximate time the message was sent
We'll investigate and take action, which may include removing messages and banning accounts.
How do I delete my account and data?
Send an email to support@slafiy.com from the email address associated with your Google account. We'll confirm the deletion request and remove all your data (account, matches, chat messages) within 30 days.
The app crashed / I found a bug
Sorry about that! Email support@slafiy.com with:
  • Device model (e.g. Samsung Galaxy A26 or iPhone 14)
  • OS version (Android 14 / iOS 17)
  • App version (found at the bottom of the home screen)
  • Steps to reproduce the bug
Screenshots help a lot. We fix reported issues in priority order.
How can I request a feature?
We love hearing from players. Email support@slafiy.com with "Feature Request" in the subject. We read every suggestion and prioritize the most requested ones for future updates.
Is my data safe?
Yes. We use Google Firebase which encrypts data in transit and at rest. We don't sell your data or show you ads. See our Privacy Policy for full details on what we collect and how we use it.

Privacy Policy

This policy describes how Slafiy collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our apps and services.

Effective: April 15, 2025
Last updated: April 15, 2025

Overview

Slafiy ("we", "our", or "us") operates mobile applications and digital services (the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding any information collected through our Apps.

Short version: Our apps are designed with privacy by default. We minimize data collection and prioritize your privacy.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our apps are built with privacy-first principles. We do not collect:

  • → Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • → Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • → Location data
  • → Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • → Camera, microphone, or contacts access
  • → Social media information

In-App Purchases

Some of our apps offer optional in-app purchases. All payment transactions are processed entirely by:

  • → Google Play Billing (Android)
  • → Apple App Store (iOS)

Slafiy does not receive, process, or store any payment card details or financial information. Purchase records are managed solely by Google and Apple under their respective privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may allow users to share results via third-party platforms (WhatsApp, Telegram, X, Instagram). This sharing is entirely optional and initiated by you. When you choose to share, the content is handled by the respective third-party platform under their own privacy policies. Slafiy does not transmit any data to these platforms independently.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our applications contain no advertising, no account creation, and no data collection of any kind, making them safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since our apps do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no user data stored on our servers. All game state and preferences are managed locally on your device and through the respective app store's secure infrastructure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
